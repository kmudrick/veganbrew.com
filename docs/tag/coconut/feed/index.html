I mixed up the ingredients in a bowl, then stirred in the coconut flakes until they were mostly evenly coated.Â  I let this sit to soak for about 15 minutes.Â  I then dumped the bowl into a frying pan over medium-high heat.Â  At this point, there was a decent amount of extra liquid in the pan, so I was aiming to carmalize that up a bit.Â  You’ll need to watch this like a hawk – it can easily burn if you step away for any substantial amount of time.Â  I give the pan a good stir every minute or so to make sure all the flakes were cooking nicely.Â  As the flakes got crispier and darker, I lowered the heat (gradually) to prevent burning.Â  Feel free to sample a bit or two along the way to see how the taste is progressing.
